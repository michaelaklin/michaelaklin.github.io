I am associate professor of economics and holder of the Chair of Policy & Sustainability ([PASU]) at EPFL – the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Lausanne. I am also Managing Director of [Enterprise for Society] (E4S), a joint center between EPFL, IMD, and the University of Lausanne.

I study the economics and politics of sustainable and steady development and the policies to achieve it.

To learn more about ongoing research, please check the [PASU Lab] page.

Our group is always keen on working with civil society, industry, and policymakers. Don’t hesitate to send me an email if you want to collaborate ([michael.aklin@epfl.ch]).
