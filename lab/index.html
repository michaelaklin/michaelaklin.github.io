Research

The Policy & Sustainability (PASU) group analyzes obstacles to long-term sustainable economic development. It has particular strengths in areas related to energy, environmental, and development issues such as:

  • Energy access, poverty, and inequality

  • Design of energy policy

  • Public opinion and climate change

  • Adoption and impact assessment of clean technology

  • Labor market reforms and decarbonization

  • Green finance, green bonds

It uses a wide range of tools to study these questions, ranging from randomized controlled trials (RCTs) to computational methods.
