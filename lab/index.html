Research

The Policy & Sustainability group analyzes obstacles to long-term economic development. It is particularly active in the following areas:

  • Energy/environmental economics and policy

  • Political economy and development

  • Sustainable technologies and innovations

Openings

New PhD students are typically recruited every year. We welcome spontaneous applications; however, we will only reply to applications that will be further considered.

Ideal profile:

  • Education. Master’s degree in economics, quantitative social sciences (political science, public policy, sociology), or related field.

  • Technical skills. Excellent quantitative skills (including causal inference) and coding skills (R, Python, Stata, etc.).

  • Experience. Research and/or professional experience is desirable, especially when it relates to the areas of focus of the Chair.

  • Language. Fluency in English is expected; knowledge of French, German or Italian is not required.

Note that all candidates must apply and be accepted by the EPFL Doctoral Program (deadlines: January 15 and April 15; the program starts on September 15). Nevertheless, if you are interested in joining the Chair, you are welcome to send your file to michael.aklin@epfl.ch for a pre-evaluation. If you wish to do so, please include the following documents (in pdf):

  • A short cover letter that includes a short description of research interests.

  • CV

  • Transcripts

  • Names and contacts of three references
